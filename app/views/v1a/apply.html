Personal Independence Payment (PIP)

Before you start

Thank you for choosing to aplpy for Personal Independence Payment (PIP) online.

It may help your application if you provide some additional information to help DWP understand how your condition affects you. You will be able to uplaod any supporting documents online during the application.

Examples of helpful information are:

  • Hospital reports.
  • GP reports.
  • MRI scans
  • Care plans.
  • Prescription lists.

Don't send any information that doesn't tell us anything about your condition. E.G. Hospital appointment letters.
