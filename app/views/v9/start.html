Apply for Personal Independence Payment (PIP)

Only use this service if you have called the PIP application phone line and been invited to apply for PIP online.

Use this service to:

  • apply for PIP
  • describe how your health condition or disability affects your ability to do every tasks and get around
  • upload any supporting documentary evidence of your condition or disability and how it affects you

Before you start

You do not have to answer all the questions in one go. You can save your progress at any point and continue later.

You will need:

  • your personal details including National Insurance number
  • details of your health conditions and disabilities and how they affect your ability to do everyday tasks and get around
  • details of any medicines or treatments you have
  • details of your health care professionals
  • hospital or care home details if you're staying or living in one
  • documentary evidence of your conditions or disabilities, such as doctor's reports, care or treatment plans, test results or repeat prescription lists
