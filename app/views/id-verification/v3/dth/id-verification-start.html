We need to ask some questions to prove your identity

You’ll need to choose 2 things we can ask you about:

  • your valid UK passport
  • a payslip dated after 17 April 2020, or your most recent P60
  • a recent Self Assessment tax return
  • your tax credit claims - this can include Voice ID
  • your credit record - for example, the year you took out a credit card or phone contract.

If you cannot answer the questions, you can confirm your identity another way.

We ask these questions to protect against fraud.
