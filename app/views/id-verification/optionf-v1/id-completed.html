What to do next

We will send your information to a health professional to consider before we make our decision. If we need more information about how your health condition or disability affects your daily life we may invite you to attend an assessment.

If you have uploaded any supporting evidence

We will include this in your claim to PIP and will consider this before we make our decision.

If you need to upload more supporting evidence

If you need to upload any more evidence that might support your claim you will need to sign in.

You will need your email address, mobile phone and password to sign in to PIP.

You will not need to answer the questions about your daily living activities or mobility again.

If anything changes or you need help

Please contact the Department for Work and Pensions (DWP) if any of the details you gave us have changed or if you need help with your claim.

If you do not want to continue with your PIP claim, please let us know.
