Apply for Personal Independence Payment (PIP)

Use this service to apply for PIP.

You should only use this service if the Department for Work and Pensions (DWP) has asked you to.

What to expect

You will be asked to:

  • answer a few basic questions to check if you’re eligible for PIP
  • provide some personal details
  • confirm your identity
  • create a password so you can save your progress
  • give details about how your health condition or disability affects you

After you apply

DWP will look at your application to make a decsion.

You might be invited to an assessment appointment to make sure DWP has enough information about you to make a decsion on your application.

Once DWP has all the information it needs to make a decision it will write to you to let you know if you’re entitled to PIP and how much you will get.
