Apply for Personal Independence Payment (PIP)

Before you start

It may help your application if you provide some supporting evidence to help the Department for Work and Pensions understand how your condition or disability affects you. You'll be able to upload any supporting evidence documents online during the application.

Examples of helpful information are:

  • hospital reports
  • GP reports
  • MRI scans
  • care plans
  • prescription lists

Do not send any information that does not tell us anything about your condition such as hospital appointment letters.
