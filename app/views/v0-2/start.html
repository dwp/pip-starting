Check if you're eligible for PIP

Use this service to check if you're eligible to and apply for PIP.

What to expect

  1. You'll be advised whether or not you may be eligible to apply. If you're not eligible we will tell you why and what else might help you.
  2. If you're eligible to apply you'll be asked to fill in some further questions about your conditions and how they affect your daily life. You may also be asked to upload some supporting information such as a hospital report.
  3. When you have answered all the questions you will be able to check your answers before submitting your application.
  4. The Department for Work and Pensions will consider your application. You might be invited to a health assessment if there is not enough information in your application.
  5. You will receive a decision in the post. If you disagree with the decision you can ask for it to be looked at again.
