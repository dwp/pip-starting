Get help

You can ask a friend, family member, or charity or support organisation to help you make your claim.

Contact the Department for Work and Pensions (DWP) if:

  • you need more time to complete your claim
  • you do not want to claim online and need a paper form
  • you need to post any evidence to support your claim
