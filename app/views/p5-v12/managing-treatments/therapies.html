Therapies you take at home with help from another person

We need to know:

  • what the therapy is
  • how often you receive the therapy
  • how long the therapy takes
  • the type of assistance you need from another person and for how long
  • tell us about any help you feel you need but do not get

Example

“I need home dialysis for 3 hours on Monday and Friday each week. My partner helps me set up the dialysis equipment and supervises me during the dialysis to make sure I stay safe.”
