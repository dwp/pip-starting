Motability Scheme

The Motability Scheme lets disabled people lease a car, scooter or powered wheelchair using all or some of their PIP mobility payments.

The scheme is operated by an independent commercial company.

If you would like more information we will share your name and address with the company that operates the scheme.

If eligible, would you like the Motability Scheme to post you information about it?

If you change your mind about receiving Motability Scheme information contact the Department for Work and Pensions (DWP).
