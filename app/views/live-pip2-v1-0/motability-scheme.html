Motability Scheme

The Motability Scheme allows disabled people to lease a car, scooter or powered wheelchair in exchange for all or some of their PIP mobility payments.

If you're eligible to join the Motability Scheme would you like us to post you information about it?

We will not share your personal details with the Motability Scheme.

If you decide you do not want to receive information about Motability in the future, contact:

Telephone: 0800 121 4433
Textphone: 0800 121 4493 if you have speech or hearing difficulties
