Tell us how your conditions or disabilities affect you

Use this stage of your claim to:

  • tell us all the conditions and disabilities you have
  • add details of health and care professionals you have not already told us about
  • describe how your conditions or disabilities affect your ability to do certain everyday tasks and get around
  • upload any supporting evidence of your conditions or disabilities and how they affect you

Take your time and give as much information as you can. You do not have to answer all the questions in one go. You can save your progress at any point and continue later.

Before you start

You will need:

  • details of your health conditions or disabilities and how they affect your ability to do everyday tasks and get around
  • details of any medicines or treatments you have
  • details of your health and care professionals
  • evidence of your conditions or disabilities if you have it, such as doctors' reports, care or treatment plans, test results or repeat prescription lists
