Preparing food

Preparing food means making a simple, one course meal for one. This includes:

  • opening tins
  • cooking or heating food on a standard hob or using a microwave

We need to know if you have any difficulties doing this:

  • safley
  • to an acceptable standard
  • as often as you need to
  • in a reasonable time - about twice the time it might take someone who does not have a disability
