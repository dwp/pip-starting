Everyday tasks and getting around

Personal Independance Payment (PIP) can help with extra living costs if you have both:

  • a long term physical or mental health condition or disability
  • difficulties doing certain everyday tasks or getting around because of your condition or disability

We will assess how difficult you find the following tasks:

  • Preparing food
  • Eating and drinking
  • Managing medicines and treatments
  • Washing and bathing
  • Using the toilet and managing incontinence
  • Dressing and undressing
  • Talking, listening and understanding
  • Reading
  • Mixing with other people
  • Managing money
  • Planning and following a journey
  • Moving around
