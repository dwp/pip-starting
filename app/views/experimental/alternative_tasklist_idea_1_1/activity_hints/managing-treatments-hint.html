Managing medicines and treatments

This means:

  • monitoring changes in your health conditions or disabilities, for example, your blood sugar level, mental state or pain levels
  • taking medication in the right way and at the right time
  • managing therapies at home which have been recommended by a health professional or pharmacist, for example, physiotherapy or home dialysis
