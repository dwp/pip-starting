Make a claim

Before you start you will need:

  • your contact details, for example a telephone number
  • your date of birth
  • your National Insurance number
  • your bank or building society account number and sort code
  • your doctor's or health worker's name, address and telephone number
  • dates and addresses of any time you have spent in a care home or in hospital
  • dates for any time you spent abroad for more than 4 weeks at a time, and the countries you visited
