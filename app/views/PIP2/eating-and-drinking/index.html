Eating and drinking

This means:

  • remembering when to eat
  • cutting food into pieces
  • putting food and drink in your mouth
  • chewing and swallowing food and drink

Consider if you need help cutting up food, getting it to your mouth or if someone needs to prompt, encourage or remind you to eat.
