Managing your money

This means:

  • understanding how much things cost
  • understanding how much change you should get
  • managing your own budgets, paying your own bills and planning your own future purchases

This does not mean taking a bill to the post box or being able to hold your change.

This also does not mean being able to read a bill or physically open a letter.
