Planning and following journeys

This means:

  • leaving your home to go out
  • following a route you know well
  • using public transport timetables to travel to somewhere new
  • working out a new route and following it, for example, if you find a road is closed on a route you know well and need to plan and follow a different route

This question is not about if you can physically move around.
