Understanding and completing this claim

We understand that some people might find it difficult to complete their claim due to the following conditions:

  • Mental health conditions – for example, depression, anxiety, post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD), obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD), psychosis, schizophrenia, bipolar or personality disorder
  • Behavioural conditions – for example, attention deficit hyperactivity disorder (ADHD) or conduct disorder
  • Learning difficulties – for example, Down’s syndrome or fragile X syndrome
  • Developmental disorders – for example, autistic spectrum disorder, developmental delay, speech or language disorders
  • Memory problems – for example, due to a brain injury, or any form of dementia like Alzheimer’s or vascular dementia
