Get help

If you need someone to help you fill in this claim or to read any letters we send, you could ask a friend, family member or support organisation. If you need help using this service contact:

Telephone: 0800 121 6279

Relay UK (if you cannot hear or speak on the phone): 18001 then 0800 121 6279

Get help

You can ask a friend, family member, or charity or support organisation to help you make your claim.

Contact the Department for Work and Pensions (DWP) if:

  • you need more time to complete your claim
  • you do not want to claim online and need a paper form
  • you need to post any evidence to support your claim
